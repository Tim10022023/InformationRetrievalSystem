The Hesshütte is named after the Gesäusepionier Heinrich Hess and is already looking back on a long tradition. Due to its popularity, the place in the original hut was no longer sufficient and a new building took place in 1996. The cozy hut atmosphere was preserved.

Reservations for rooms and mattresses best online or by phone at +436644308060.
